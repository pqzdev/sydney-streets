# Geographic Scope: Greater Sydney

## Official Definition

This project uses the **NSW Government's official definition of Greater Sydney** as defined by the [Greater Sydney Commission](https://www.greater.sydney/) and the [Environmental Planning and Assessment (Greater Sydney Region Districts) Order 2017](https://www.planningportal.nsw.gov.au/opendata/dataset/planning-district-boundaries).

## Coverage

Greater Sydney comprises **33 Local Government Areas (LGAs)** organized into **5 planning districts**:

### 1. Eastern City District (9 LGAs)
- Bayside
- Burwood
- Canada Bay
- Inner West
- City of Randwick
- Strathfield
- City of Sydney
- Waverley
- Woollahra

### 2. Central City District (4 LGAs)
- City of Blacktown
- Cumberland
- City of Parramatta
- The Hills Shire

### 3. Western City District (8 LGAs)
- Camden
- City of Campbelltown
- City of Blue Mountains
- City of Fairfield
- City of Hawkesbury
- City of Liverpool
- City of Penrith
- Wollondilly

### 4. North District (9 LGAs)
- Hornsby
- Hunter's Hill
- Ku-ring-gai
- Lane Cove
- Mosman
- North Sydney
- Northern Beaches
- City of Ryde
- City of Willoughby

### 5. South District (3 LGAs)
- Canterbury-Bankstown
- Georges River
- Sutherland

## Geographic Extent

Greater Sydney stretches:
- **North**: Hawkesbury and Northern Beaches
- **West**: Blue Mountains
- **South**: Wollondilly, Campbelltown, and Sutherland

## Data Filtering

When loading street data, we filter to include only streets within these 33 LGAs. This ensures our analysis focuses on Greater Sydney as officially defined for metropolitan planning purposes.
